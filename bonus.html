Alabama Big AI

The mascot known as "Big Al" today was the brainchild of University of Alabama student Walt Tart, member of Tau Kappa Epsilon fraternity.

Fun Facts

  1. Big AI looks like an Elephant
  2. Big Al is generally not allowed to interact on the field with opponent mascots
  3. Hold your breath, the elephants are coming quotes are framed before football match
